Challenge

The lack of study material such as textbooks, pens and paper is one of the many challenges standing in the way of a good educational system in Ethiopia. Low income combined with the limited assets, discourages parents to send their children to school because its expensive. The consequences of parents being discouraged to send their children to school are extremely damaging to their development for generations to come.

Risks:

  • Breach of privacy
  • Low participation rates
  • Economy
